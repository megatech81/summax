Smart Inventory Management System

This project involves creating an AI-driven inventory management system for retail businesses. The system uses machine learning algorithms to predict stock requirements based on historical sales data, seasonal trends, and current market analysis. It integrates with existing POS systems and supply chain management tools to provide real-time inventory tracking and automatic reordering. The goal is to optimize stock levels, reduce overstocking or stockouts, and improve overall inventory efficiency.
